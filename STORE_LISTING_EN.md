## 4. Full Store Description (Copy-Paste)

PhotoAura Studio is a powerful professional photo editing and digital design suite optimized for Windows 10 and 11, designed to deliver a streamlined, out-of-the-box creative workflow.

### Key Features:
* **Photoshop Keyboard Shortcuts:** Move seamlessly between apps without losing muscle memory. Uses standard industry hotkeys for layer duplication, free transform, selection tools, and more.
* **Workspace Optimization:** Pre-configured with a highly productive single-window layout. Tools on the left, active options on the top-left, and layers, brushes, and history organized neatly on the right.
* **Deep Velvet Violet Theme:** A premium, modern dark visual theme designed to minimize eye strain and maximize color focus.
* **Symbolic Flat Icons:** High-contrast, clean vector monochrome icons that provide a professional, precise, and clutter-free interface.
* **Google Fonts Library:** Preloaded cache of highly popular typography fonts ready to use immediately.
* **Secure Portable Mode:** Fully isolated sandbox run environment that will not interfere with other local software installations.
